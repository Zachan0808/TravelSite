Company History

Established in 1972, TravelBloom has blossomed into one of the industry's leading travel agencies. Founded by visionaries John and Emily Winters, the company began in a small Los Angeles office, offering airline bookings and package tours. Over the decades, TravelBloom expanded nationwide, earning a reputation for exceptional customer service and innovative travel experiences. The Winters siblings constantly sought out new destinations, unique offerings, and cutting-edge technologies to enhance the travel journey. In the 1990s, TravelBloom embraced the digital revolution, launching a user-friendly website that streamlined online booking. This digital transformation opened the company up to a global audience. Further growth came through strategic acquisitions, allowi,ng TravelBloom to diversify its services and cater to a wider range of travelers. Today, the company operates over 50 offices and boasts a team of skilled travel experts. Committed to innovation, personalization, and customer satisfaction, TravelBloom continues to push the boundaries of travel. From luxury safaris to family cruises, the company inspires travelers to explore the world in extraordinary ways. With a rich history and bold vision, TravelBloom is poised for new journeys ahead.
